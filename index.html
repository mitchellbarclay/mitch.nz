Graphic/ illustrative

As the lead designer at StudyTime, I took the helm in crafting a distinctive design language that focused on promoting accessible and stress-free education. My role encompassed creating social media graphics, designing workbooks, and illustrating for both digital and print resources. Through the use of vibrant, friendly visuals, I sought to reduce the intimidation often associated with learning, making the educational content more approachable and engaging.

As the principal graphic designer at the New Zealand Veterinary Association, my role is to produce visual content that supports our multifaceted objectives. This includes designing practical resources for clinics, generating marketing collateral for our events, and aiding in campaigns that address the veterinarian shortage in New Zealand. Through a process of ideation, review, and refinement, my designs aim to emphasize the organization's priorities, promote member engagement, and contribute to our mission of supporting and advocating for veterinarians.
